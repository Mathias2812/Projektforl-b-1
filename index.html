10 Scary Facts!

Only 9% of all plastic produced is recycled.

A whopping 2 million plastic bags are used every minute worldwide.

100 million marine animals die each year from plastic waste alone.

One million plastic bottles are bought every minute.

Nearly one-quarter of the world's plastic waste is mismanaged or littered.

A plastic bottle can last for 450 years in the marine environment, slowly
fragmenting into smaller and smaller pieces which never truly disappear.

There will be more plastic in our oceans than fish by 2050.

90% of plastic is made from fossil fuels.

It takes 22 gallons of water to make one pound of plastic.

If everyone switched to reusable bottles and carton water, we will
save enough energy to power New York City for an entire month.
